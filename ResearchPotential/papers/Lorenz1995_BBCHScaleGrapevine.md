## Summary

Codified the BBCH scale for grapevine phenological stages. The phenological development is divided into principal growth stages (0-9) and secondary growth stages (0-9), with a two-digit code for each growth stage.

## Key Stages Referenced in the Research Plan

- **BBCH 07:** Budburst (beginning of bud burst)
- **BBCH 65:** Full bloom (50% of flowerhoods fallen)
- **BBCH 85:** Softening of berries (veraison)
- **BBCH 89:** Berries ripe for harvest

## Relevance to Tokaj Research

The standard protocol for recording phenological observations needed for Manuscript 5 (GFV model calibration). Multi-year phenological data at BBCH stages 07, 65, 85, 89 at representative plots is required for calibrating Furmint and Harslevelyu parameters.
