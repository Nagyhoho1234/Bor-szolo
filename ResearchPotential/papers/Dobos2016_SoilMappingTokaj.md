## Summary

Applied regression kriging for soil property mapping in the Tokaj region. Follow-up to Pasztor et al. (2015) digital terroir mapping. Used automated classification algorithms and soil diagnostic property maps as regionalized qualifiers.

## Key Context

- Part of broader Tokaj terroir characterization project
- Regression kriging applied to create continuous soil property maps
- Category-type soil maps compiled by classification trees method
- Most relevant soil properties: drainage, water holding capacity, soil depth, pH

## Relevance to Tokaj Research

Provides existing soil property maps for Tokaj that can be integrated with the 50-station climate data for comprehensive terroir analysis and water balance modeling (Direction 5).
