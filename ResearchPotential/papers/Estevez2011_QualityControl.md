## Abstract

Quality assurance procedures applied to meteorological data from the Agroclimatic Information Network of Andalusia (Southern Spain). Includes validation of record structure, range/limits, time and internal consistency, persistence and spatial consistency tests.

## QC Pipeline (from the research plan)

1. **Range tests:** Flag values outside physically plausible or climatological bounds
2. **Step tests:** Flag implausible jumps between consecutive readings (e.g., >15C change in daily Tmean)
3. **Persistence tests:** Flag runs of identical values indicating sensor failure
4. Battery voltage checks for systematic measurement errors

## Relevance to Tokaj Research

The standard reference for building the three-tier QC pipeline for the 50-station network data. Essential first step before any analysis.
